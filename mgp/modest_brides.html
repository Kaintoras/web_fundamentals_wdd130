Modest Brides

All our dresses are carefully thought to offer you everything you want and need from a wedding dress. We firmly believe that a wedding dress is the most special dress you will ever wear, so to make it the best we can only work with the best materials. From high quality fabrics to meticulous patterns and details that will leave you and your guests speechless. A wedding dress is synonym of haute couture for us. We have a variety of shapes (mermaid, princess, imperial,), fabrics (crepe, satin, Mikado,…)and styles (modern, boohoo, chic,…). You can have a look at some of our dresses and check if there is one of your liking. If you don't find something you can still contact us and we will arrange a meeting with you to understand what you want and we will design a few options for you. After choosing the design you will be able to see a catalogue of fabrics and materials (embroideries, buttons,…) and we will start working on your dress. There will be multiple fittings so that your dress becomes only yours! Once you are satisfied with the dress we will make sure it goes through dry cleaning and ironing and you will be ready to go!
