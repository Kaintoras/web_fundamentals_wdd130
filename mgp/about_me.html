About Me

I am a Venezuelan designer based in Madrid. When I was young, I learned the basics of garments alterations thanks to my mom and grandmother. I started later my studies on fashion design in Madrid and continued to study in London. The bridal industry was always something I admired and so I went on to look for jobs in that field. I have worked with designers like Carli Pearson, Jordi Dalmau, Silvia Fernandez and so on. During my time working in the field and being a bride myself I realised the lack of modest options available and decided it was time to change that. I started my own brand with the idea of making easier what was difficult for me and is still difficult for a lot of girls all over the world. Come with me and join me in the bridal revolution!!
